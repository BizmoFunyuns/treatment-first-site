OxyContin Addiction

OxyContin is the brand name of the time release drug known as oxycodone. Given street names such as Kicker, Cottin, OCs, 40 Bar, and Oxy. Classed as an opiate, it is prescribed to combat moderate to severe physical pain in patients. As it is a ‘controlled substance’ it has a very high risk for addiction and dependence. Respiratory failure and death can occur when taken in high doses, or when mixed with other substances, especially alcohol.

Your Sober Journey works with OxyContin addicts to the best of our ability to help you, or a loved one start their journey to a new life. We will work with you to develop a treatment program plan based around one of the dozens of facilities we are partnered with. We will set up your assessment, work with you or a family member to get the help you deserve to free yourself from OxyContin abuse.
