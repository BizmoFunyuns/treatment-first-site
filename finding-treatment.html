Finding Treatment
Eliminating Your Obstacles

We know the issues that arise when searching for the right treatment center. Leave the hours of research, phone calls, insurance policies, and travel arrangement costs up to us. Relax, let us help you get where you deserve to be.

Getting the Details
Location

We will need to determine if you’d like to find treatment in state, or out of state. Your preference is our preference, we will do all we can to find a program that will provide you with what you need.

Treatment Options

Using our connections, we will scout out a number of pre screened facilities to help choose the right fit for you.

Insurance

Using any insurance benefits, we scan many reputable facilities to choose programs that will be the most financially beneficial for you.

The Process

We will guide you through what you should do prior to leaving for treatment, what you should bring to treatment, and what you should expect upon arrival. Whether you’re flying out of state, or staying in state, we can help set up your assessment.
