Drug Rehabilitation
Understanding Drug Addiction

We have the vision to help treat addicts that use any of the broad spectrum of drugs, including prescription drugs, club drugs, cocaine, and heroin. Many addicts use and abuse multiple drugs, and need a specialized program that will support them correctly through detox. As any drug takes over the addicts life, the user can lose their life piece by piece. Family, home, and occupations get consumed by the addiction until their is nothing left. Physical well being drastically changes as consumption worsens, changes to the brain occur, respiratory failure, organ failure, and erratic behavioral outlashes on life around the addict. The negative effects of drug addiction hollows out an individual, and creates vast emptiness within a family.

Common Types of Drug Addiction:

  • Treatment patients who develop a dependency on pain relief prescriptions such as OxyContin, Vicodin or other hydrocodones/oxycodones.
  • Recreational or experimental drug users who switched to cocaine, heroin, or PCP when their previous drug couldn't provide that all time “high” they continue to search for.
  • Patients prescribed stimulants, or depressants who develop the need for larger doses to achieve the effects of the drug. Such as codeine, methamphetamine, benzodiazepine, hydrocodones, and oxycodones.
  • Teens and young adults who develop an addiction over time from from using club drugs. These drugs include Molly, MDMA, ecstasy, methamphetamine, Rohypnol, and GHB.
  • Any addict who began by escaping reality, and self medicating with any available prescription, or street drug.

We understand the underlying causes of any addiction, and are available 24/7 to answer to your needs when questioning, and creating your treatment plan. It's not too late, don't wait until it is.
