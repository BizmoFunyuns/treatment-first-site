Travel Arrangements
Sending You On Your Journey

If you have chosen a program out of state, we will make all of your travel arrangements for you. Upon your flights arrival, we will have arranged for you to be picked up by your treatment facility, making sure you get taken to your destination safely. If you are staying in state, we can have ground transportation arranged for you if needed, no worries. We are dedicated to making this process as relaxing and worry free for you as possible.

Travel Services

  • Arrange your flight for you
  • Have a shuttle for you upon flight arrival to take you to your destination
  • If you are staying in state, or prefer not to fly, we can arrange convenient ground transportation for you
  • Upon arrival at your facility, if chosen to, we can notify family that you have arrived safely
