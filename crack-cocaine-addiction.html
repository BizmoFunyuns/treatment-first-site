Crack Cocaine Addiction

Crack cocaine is a concentrated form of pure cocaine, it is formulated by dissolving powdered cocaine in a solution of water and sodium bicarbonate. When the solution is boiled, the drug solidifies into “rocks” which are then broken up to sell. When smoked, this drug produces a short lived immediate high, lasting around 5-10 minutes. These short highs result in frequent use of the drug, and high addiction rates. Users are at risk for heart and respiratory failure, strokes, hallucinations, and convulsions. Over time, addiction to this drug deteriorates the life of the addict from the inside and out, being unable to fulfill any obligations due to dependency of the drug.

Your Sober Journey works with crack cocaine addicts to the best of our ability to help you, or a loved one start their journey to a new life. We will work with you to develop a treatment program plan based around one of the dozens of facilities we are partnered with. We will set up your assessment, work with you or a family member to get the help you deserve to free yourself from crack cocaine abuse.
